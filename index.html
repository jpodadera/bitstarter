Fly with an Ar-Drone is funny. Fly with more than one Ar-Drone is better. So far to fly more than one Ar-Drone needed a control device for each Ar-Drone, and a friend to manage each device. Now with ArSquad from a single device can command an entire squadron of Ar-Drones using predefined aerobatic flight plans.

Define your own stunts chaining different predefined individual spins. All tricks are tested thoroughly predefined to conclude that they are safe for the integrity of the device and spectators.

The application includes a library of basic stunts that can be expanded by acquiring new exciting modules advanced acrobatics. The full version includes all modules stunts plus an additional module that lets you create your own moves and stunts. A professional version will support up to 10 ArDrone simultaneously.

During the execution of planning ArSquad monitors how each ARDrone executing the orders of flight and its battery. Any device with high deviations from the orders will be excluded from the implementation of planning and land gently on the ground while the other appliances continue the show with security.

Plan, test and compete with your friends while creating the best acrobatic show.
