Fly with one Ar-Drone is funny. Fly with more than one Ar-Drone is better. So far, flying more than one Ar-Drone requires a control device for each Ar-Drone, and a friend to manage each device. Now with ArSquad a single device can command a squadron of up to three Ar-Drones using predefined aerobatic flight plans.

Define your own stunts chaining different predefined individual stunts. All predefined stunts are tested thoroughly to conclude that they are safe for the integrity of Ar-Drone and spectators.

Application includes a library of basic stunts that can be expanded by acquiring new exciting modules with advanced acrobatics. The full version includes all modules stunts plus an additional module that lets you create your own moves and stunts. A professional version will support up to 6 Ar-Drones simultaneously.

During the execution of plan, ArSquad monitors battery of each Ar-Drone executing the orders of flight. Any Ar-Drone with low battery will be excluded from planning and will land gently on the ground, while show wil go on with rest of Ar-Drones.
